Key Features

  • Personalized News Feed: Get articles tailored to your interests and reading habits.
  • Bias-Free Mode: Enable neutral filtering for a more objective news experience.
  • Morning Brief: Start your day with a 5-minute daily summary of top headlines.
  • Real-Time Alerts: Stay updated with breaking news notifications relevant to you.
  • Dark Mode: Browse comfortably day or night with a sleek, dark interface.

Why We Created Newsly

In today's digital world, information overload is real. We are bombarded with headlines, opinions, and algorithms that often feed us more confusion than clarity. At Newsly, we believe news should empower, not overwhelm. That's why we built an app that curates trustworthy stories, removes clickbait, and respects your time. Whether you're on a quick break or catching up over coffee, Newsly delivers what matters—nothing more, nothing less.

Our mission is simple: to help people reconnect with factual, thoughtful journalism without the noise. We designed Newsly with you in mind—minimal, intelligent, and always in sync with your world. Thank you for joining us on this journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Newsly free to use?
    Yes! Newsly is completely free to download and use.
  • Which platforms are supported?
    Newsly is available on both Android and iOS.
  • Where does Newsly get its news?
    We aggregate news from verified and reliable sources only.
  • Can I turn off notifications?
    Yes, all push notifications are fully customizable.
  • Does Newsly support dark mode?
    Absolutely! You can switch to dark mode in the settings.
